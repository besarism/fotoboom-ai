Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Welcome to FotoBoom. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Photos and Images: Photos you choose to transform using our AI features. These are processed in real-time and are never stored on our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device information including device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics data about how you use the app, including features used and session duration.
  • Purchase Information: Transaction data related to in-app purchases, processed securely through RevenueCat.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and transform your photos using AI technology
  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Process your transactions and manage subscriptions
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Communicate with you about updates and support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Photo Processing

Your photos are processed using Google Gemini API for AI transformations. Important points about photo handling:

  • Photos are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers
  • We do not save, retain, or archive any of your photos
  • Transformed images are sent directly to your device
  • We do not use your photos for training AI models
  • Your photos remain private and are only used for the transformation you request

4. Third-Party Services

We work with trusted third-party services to provide our features:

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI-powered photo transformations. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions and purchases securely. Subject to RevenueCat's privacy policy.
  • Analytics Services: We use anonymous analytics to understand app usage and improve our services.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This includes:

  • Encrypted data transmission using HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure processing environments
  • Regular security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary:

  • Photos are never stored - processed in real-time only
  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Transaction records are kept as required by law
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Object to certain processing
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at balkans.apps@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

FotoBoom is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable laws.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of FotoBoom after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: balkans.apps@gmail.com
